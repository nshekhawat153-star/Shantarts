The Legacy Revived

From the Royal Court to the Modern High-Rise

We are the revival of a family-run heritage art house from the 1990s that exported traditional Indian paintings across the globe. Today, we bridge that rich ancestral legacy with elite modern design, offering museum-grade handmade Rajasthani paintings and luxury Indian wall art for curated global interiors.

Generations ago, our family worked in tandem with the master painters of Udaipur, Jaipur, and Nathdwara. The transition of centuries-old techniques—such as hand-grinding natural mineral stones and painting with single-squirrel-hair brushes—was a treasure passed from father to son. In reviving Shanta Arts, we preserve this authentic Indian heritage art while adapting it for sophisticated contemporary spaces.

In reviving this business, we merge museum-grade preservation with a refined editorial aesthetic. We cooperate directly with the descendent master artisans of Rajasthan to design custom, large-scale heritage artworks and custom wall murals that anchor high-end hotels, private villas, and premium commercial spaces.

The Artistry Journey

The Commission Experience

An immersive, white-glove process transforming architectural conceptualization into monumental heritage art.

01

Aesthetic Alignment & Concept

We begin with a personalized design consultation. Whether aligned with blueprints, elevations, or mood boards, we discuss your interior's scale, wall lighting, palette direction, and narrative motifs. We outline composition choices, ensuring a harmonious heritage fit.

02

Intricate Charcoal Sketching

Our master artist lays down the initial pencil and charcoal blueprint of the entire painting on coarse handmade canvas or paper. We share this initial structural geometry with you and your design team for sign-off, ensuring that proportions, motifs, and architectural alignments are perfect.

03

Mineral Painting & Gold Leafing

Natural mineral pigments—crushed malachite, indigo, saffron, lapis lazuli, and terracotta—are hand-blended. With a single-hair brush, the master artist applies paints meticulously, culminating in the application of 24k gold leaf foil accents to bring brilliant warmth and historical richness.

04

Luxury Editorial Framing

The artwork is mounted on museum-grade preservation backing. We construct bespoke solid wood frames—antique teak, gold leaf overlays, or sleek black oak—with high-transparency non-reflective museum glass to shield natural minerals from UV exposure.

05

White-Glove Worldwide Delivery

The framed or rolled canvas is placed in climate-controlled custom wooden crates. We arrange premium air-freight transport with white-glove shipping partners, tracking the custom heritage artwork from our studio directly to your global doorstep. Fully insured and safely handled.
